World's Best Chocolate Cake

Back to Home Page

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (240g) cake or AP flour;
  • 2 cups (396g) granulated sugar;
  • 3/4 cup (63g) unsweetned cocoa powder;
  • 2 teaspoons (8g) baking powder;
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons (9g) baking soda;
  • 1 teaspoon (2.8g) kosher salt;
  • 1 teaspoon (2.3g) espresso powder;
  • 1 cup (227g) buttermilk (or whole milk);
  • 1/2 cup (99g) vegetable oil;
  • 2 large eggs;
  • 2 teaspoons (9.4g) vanilla extract;
  • 1 cup (227g) boiling water;

Directions

  1. Let the oven pre-heat at 180°C (350°F) for a minimum of 10 minutes.
  2. Whisk all dry ingredients together.
  3. Add all the wet ingredients carefully (except the water) until fully combined.
  4. Carefully stir in the boiling water.
  5. Bake for about 35-40 min.
